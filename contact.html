About Me

I am an aspiring Software Engineer with interests in Application Development, Mathematics, FinTech, and Data Science. I eagerly seek growth through the cultivation of my skills and learning new technologies.

My passion is bridging the gap between the computer sciences and mathematics, from both a philosophical and technical standpoint, in a way that would encourage the engineering of clever solutions to significant problems that have an impact on the real world.

Ultimately, having both a background in Mathematics as well as Computer Science, I hope to supplement my future endeavors as a Software Engineer using my skills in Mathematics as a foundation along with a pursuit for higher education.
